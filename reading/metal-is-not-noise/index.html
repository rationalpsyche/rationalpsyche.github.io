Metal is not noise

The common perception of Metal as musical genre is of people screaming and making noise. Since I had to explain multiple times that there are Metal subgenres with clean vocals that can be appealing to a wider audience I thought it was worth a blog post.

The following are examples of metal songs with clean voice that you might be surprised to enjoy

Metal Ballads

  • Sonata Arctica - Letter to Dana
  • Sonata Arctica - Tallulah
  • Ghost - Life Eternal

“Opera Metal” / Nightwish

  • Nightwish - Nemo
  • Nightwish - Ghost Love Score

Note that Tarja was the former lead vocalist of Nightwish. She is a soprano with a three and a half octave range.

Folk Metal

  • Eluveitie - The call of the Mountains
  • Eluveitie - A Rose for Epona

Electronic

  • Pendulum - Witchcraft

Covers

  • Lillasyster - Umbrella
  • Disturbed - Land of Confusion
