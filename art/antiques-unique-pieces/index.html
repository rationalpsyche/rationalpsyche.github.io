Medusa pendant

This masterpiece, created by Philippe Wolfers, is internationally recognized as an icon of Art Noveau jewellery. The pendant will be preserved in the Brussel Museum of Art and History.




Art Noveau-style pedestal, made from bronze and fossilised wood

Victor Horta designed this piece for the winter garden of his house. Thanks to the acquisition by the Charles Vreeken Fund it returned to its original place within Horta’s house, where the Horta Museum is now located.
